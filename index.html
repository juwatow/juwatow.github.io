Hi, I'm Jeanne d'Arc Uwatowenimana, M.Sc., CSM

Software Engineer & Business Agility Advocate

A dynamic, bilingual Software Engineer who is passionate about how a team/organization making software can achieve "Great Results & Great Human Satisfaction". In 2019, after a great success in helping my team find good ways to deliver valuable features as a part-time Scrum Master, I decided to become a business agility advocate. My passion lies in helping people around me grow by balancing my time between coaching teams to become values-driven, self-organized and high-performant as well as coaching the leaders to create an environment for their teams to strive.
